The Problem

The entire world is facing several environmental issues, such as global warming, wildlife population decreases and endangerment, rising sea levels, climate change, and intensified natural disasters. Many of these issues are the results of human activity like overconstruction, deforestation and habitat destruction, overfishing, hunting, and pollution. As inhabitants of this planet, we must stand together and work to reduce the causes of environmental issues and vow to prevent activities that would harm the health of the ecosystem.
