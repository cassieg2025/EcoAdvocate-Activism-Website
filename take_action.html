Ways You Can Help

  • Learn about the issues and become aware of why the environment is in its current condition.
  • Spread your awareness to others and teach them about what you have learned. Knowledge can be a valuable tool when fighting for a specific cause.
  • Advocate for your beliefs. Email your local government representative if you are seeing active environmental impacts in your area.
  • Suggest changes to your community, such as sustainable energy devices like wind turbines and solar panels.
  • Participate and volunteer. Beach cleanups, recycling initiatives, and eco-friendly community service projects are all ways that you can help make a difference locally. Research opportunities in your local community.

Why?

Advocating for environmental protection is crucial to the future of our planet and to the health of all life on Earth. Supporting efforts to reduce hazards that threaten the environment can help minimize disastrous consequences that are already beginning to form. According to the ICUN Red List, "more than 47,000 species are threatened with extinction." This problem is dramatically threatening the ecosystem's health.

Stand together. Reduce our impact. Help create a healthy planet for all its inhabitants.
